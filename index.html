About Me

I established Lauren Todesko Bookkeeping LLC in 2020 to provide affordable bookkeeping and accounting services to small businesses in West Virginia. I have a Master's in Accounting from Grand Canyon University and a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration w/ emphasis in Human Resource Management from Fairmont State University.

Over the last eight years, I've worked a variety of roles in the bookkeeping and accounting sector, and I'm excited to use my acquired skills & knowledge to help local small businesses succeed.
